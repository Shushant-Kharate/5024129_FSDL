Top 10 Places to Visit in India

1. Agra, Uttar Pradesh

Home to the Taj Mahal, the ultimate symbol of love. While the white marble mausoleum is the main draw, don’t miss the Agra Fort and the ghost city of Fatehpur Sikri nearby.

2. Jaipur, Rajasthan

Known as the "Pink City," Jaipur is a feast of royal history and vibrant markets. Highlights include Hawa Mahal, Amer Fort, and the City Palace. Learn more at the Rajasthan Tourism website.

3. Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

One of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world. It is the spiritual heart of India, famous for the evening Ganga Aarti. Official details are available on Uttar Pradesh Tourism.

4. Kerala Backwaters (Alleppey & Kumarakom)

Often called "God's Own Country," this region is best experienced by houseboat travel. Explore more at the Kerala Tourism official website.

5. Leh-Ladakh, Ladakh

A high-altitude desert known for Himalayan landscapes, turquoise lakes, and monasteries. Visit the official Ladakh Tourism portal for details.

6. Goa

India’s beach capital, famous for nightlife, beaches, and Portuguese heritage. Discover more on the Goa Tourism official site.

7. Udaipur, Rajasthan

The "City of Lakes" is one of India’s most romantic destinations. Travel information is available at Rajasthan Tourism.

8. Hampi, Karnataka

A UNESCO World Heritage Site with ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire. Learn more at Karnataka Tourism.

9. Rishikesh, Uttarakhand

Known as the "Yoga Capital of the World," offering spirituality and adventure. Official information is available on Uttarakhand Tourism.

10. Majuli, Assam

The world’s largest river island, famous for slow travel and culture. Visit the Assam Tourism website for more details.
