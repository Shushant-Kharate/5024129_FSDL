Top 10 Places to Visit in India

1. Agra, Uttar Pradesh

Home to the Taj Mahal, the ultimate symbol of love. While the white marble mausoleum is the main draw, don’t miss the Agra Fort and the ghost city of Fatehpur Sikri nearby.

2. Jaipur, Rajasthan

Known as the "Pink City," Jaipur is a feast of royal history and vibrant markets. Highlights include the honeycomb-styled Hawa Mahal, the hilltop Amer Fort, and the City Palace.

2026 Highlight: Jaipur is reinventing itself as a modern design hub with DIY craft workshops in new "Craft Districts."

3. Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

One of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world. It is the spiritual heart of India, where you can witness the mesmerizing evening Ganga Aarti on the banks of the River Ganges.

4. Kerala Backwaters (Alleppey & Kumarakom)

Often called "God's Own Country," this region is best experienced by hiring a traditional houseboat. Float through tranquil canals while enjoying authentic Keralan cuisine.

5. Leh-Ladakh, Ladakh

A high-altitude desert known for rugged Himalayan landscapes, turquoise lakes like Pangong Tso, and ancient Buddhist monasteries.

Trend Alert: Visit the Hanle Dark Sky Reserve for world-class stargazing.

6. Goa

India's beach capital. North Goa is famous for nightlife and water sports, while South Goa offers quiet beaches like Palolem.

Don't Miss: Portuguese churches in Old Goa and lush spice plantations.

7. Udaipur, Rajasthan

The "City of Lakes" is one of India’s most romantic destinations. The Lake Palace appears to float on Lake Pichola, and sunset boat rides are unforgettable.

8. Hampi, Karnataka

A UNESCO World Heritage site featuring ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire, with massive boulders and intricately carved temples like the Vittala Temple.

9. Rishikesh, Uttarakhand

The "Yoga Capital of the World," offering meditation retreats and adventure sports like white-water rafting on the Ganges.

10. Majuli, Assam

The world's largest river island, ideal for slow travel. Known for Neo-Vaishnavite culture, traditional mask-making, and eco-friendly bamboo stays.
